About

I’m a solo acoustic singer-guitarist performing around Bribie Island and the Moreton Bay region. My sets are built around acoustic interpretations of well-loved songs — from classic rock and 90s favourites through to modern country and laid-back acoustic tracks.

My focus is simple: create a warm, welcoming atmosphere where guests feel relaxed and the music supports the moment — whether that’s a private party, small wedding, café, market or community event.

I provide all my own professional sound equipment, keep communication clear and make the process easy from first enquiry to the last song of the day.

If you’re looking for chilled, heartfelt live music without the ego or noise — you’re in the right place.
